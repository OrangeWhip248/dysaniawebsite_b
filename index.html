Welcome to Dysania
Welcome to Dysania! We are a political roleplay server containing many fun features, such as guns, vehicles, and more! Aerial shot of Greenwater from Dysania 3.0. Credit: Deli_Manager.
Dysania is made up of countries, much like the real world. There are many nations on Dysania. Many countries are open to accepting new members, so just check ask if you are unsure of where to go. Bakuthemooneater's 100 gecs Machine from Dysania 5.0. Credit: mejackmac.
If you would like to start your own nation, feel free to do that too. Many nations are also open to alliances with new nations, which can be extremely beneficial to your newfound country. A vehicle showcase in the End Dimension of Dysania 3.0. Credit: mejackmac.
If you have not already joined our discord server, please do so. You must join the discord server in order to begin the vetting process. A group of players in front of a house on Dysania 3.0. Some players are using tank vehicles. Credit: mejackmac.
