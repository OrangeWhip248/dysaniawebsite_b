Discord Rules
  1. Please be nice to each other and do not be toxic. Please keep the server fun for everyone and avoid insulting or being rude to each other. Remember, this is just a block game.
  2. Do not harass anybody. This includes staff members. Again, please be nice.
  3. Please use each channel for its proper purpose. This is in an effort to keep the server organized and to keep conversations on topic.
  4. Please do not use words or phrases that others may find rude or offensive, such as racial slurs. This helps keep the server a more enjoyable place for everyone.
  5. Please do not discuss real-life politics, religion, or other sensitive social topics on our Discord server. The server is much more enjoyable for everyone if we avoid these topics and avoid the arugements that spur from them.
  6. Please do not post NSFW content in the server. Posting this type of content will result in you being banned.
  7. Do NOT bring alt accounts onto our Discord server. Alt accounts are annoying and just cause a headache for the moderation team.
  8. Please invite new users using the Discord or website link and not the server ip. This is to ensure that they can go through the proper vetting process, just like everyone else.
  9. Please only invite people who you believe will be good fits for the server. Make sure they will understand the core concepts and rules of the server before sending the invite. If you wish to publicly advertise or advertise on another discord server, please ask the Admins beforehand.
  10. Do not advertise anything that is not related to the game on the Discord server.
  11. Spam is not allowed. Breaking this rule may result in a mute or tempban.
  12. Do NOT encourage others to break the rules of the discord or Minecraft server. Encouraging rule breaking by others will be treated as if you are breaking the rule yourself.
  13. Please remember that Staff have the final decision in all moderation-related matters.
